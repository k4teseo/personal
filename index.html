About Me

Hi, I'm Kate Seo! I'm a rising junior at Boston University studying computer science with a minor in innovation & entrepreneurship. With a fresh perspective honed by my upbringing in New York, I bring creativity and attention to detail to all things product, design, and engineering.


I'm passionate about tech because it allows people of all backgrounds to connect with others and bring a unique vision to life. In the future I hope to work as a product manager, software engineer, or UX designer at a company that works to make tech accessible to all.


At Boston University I serve as the marketing director for Hack4Impact, a club that creates social good through building projects for non-profits. I'm also the project manager of community at Spark!, where I work to build relationships and create an inclusive environment for an organization of 200+ students in tech. Outside of school I love to travel and am studying abroad in London this upcoming semester. I've always been interested in learning about new places and cultures and am currently teaching myself Chinese to broaden my skill set. Thank you for visiting my site!
